ALIUS Bulletin n°6 (2022-2024) 
edited by Alessio Bucci, Lena Coutrot, George Fejer,
Daniel A. Friedman & ​Matthieu Koroma

Contents 
​
Anna Ciaunica (recorded by Daniel Friedman and edited by Lena Coutrot and Matthieu Koroma)
What is like to be in early sensory life, 3 pp.

Steven Jillings (recorded by Daniel Friedman and edited by Lena Coutrot and Matthieu Koroma)
What is like to be in weightlessness, 5 pp.

Matthieu Koroma (recorded by Daniel Friedman and edited by Lena Coutrot and Matthieu Koroma)
What is like to be asleep, 5 pp.

Juan González (interviewed by Matthieu Koroma)
An interdisciplinary journey into consciousness research, 18 pp.
​
Robert Sapolsky (interviewed by Daniel Friedman & Alexandra Mikhailova)
On free will or the lack thereof, 11 pp.

Manoj Doss (interviewed by George Fejer)
On the Cinco Sins of Psychedelic Research, 27 pp.

Jeremy I. Skipper (interviewed by Leor Roseman, edited by Matthieu Koroma & George Fejer )
The intimacy of psychedelics, language, and consciousness, 30 pp.
