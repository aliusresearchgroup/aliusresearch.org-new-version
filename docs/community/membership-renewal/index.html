Annual Membership Renewal Fee

​To ensure the continued growth and operational efficiency of our association, we require members to pay an annual membership fee. This fee is essential for maintaining our website and covering other necessary running costs. All funds and expenditures are transparently managed and subject to member discussion and vote during our bi-annual meetings. These meetings provide a platform for members to propose and decide on budget allocations, such as organizing in-person events or supporting scientific initiatives. Your contribution helps sustain ALIUS.

Ability to pay tier system

Our payment tier system is designed to ensure fairness and accessibility, allowing members to contribute according to their financial abilities. Membership fees are entirely voluntary, and members can choose to contribute as much or as little as they wish. To provide guidance, we suggest three tiers based on different financial capacities.
0 - €20
Early Career Tier
  • ​Individuals with low income
  • Uncertain academic position
  • Members who have significantly contributed through their time and effort.
€50
Advanced Career Tier
  • Individuals with some disposable income
  • In an academic position for the near future
€100
Institutional Tier
  • Individuals who can reimburse the cost through institutional funding.
  • Principal Investigators, professors, associate professors, tenured professors etc.
