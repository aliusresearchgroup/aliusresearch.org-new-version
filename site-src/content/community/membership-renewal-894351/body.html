How to become an ALIUS member?

There are two paths to ALIUS membership:

Voting Membership


Active Role


  • ​Career building opportunity  for undergraduate students and early-career researchers!
  • Full voting rights
  • Reduced annual membership fees

Researcher 


  • Core network of researchers who study non-ordinary states of consciousness
  • Participate in symposia, journal clubs and networking events
  • Voting rights in all policies of ALIUS

Content Access


Supporter


  • Hold your hands in the flame for ALIUS by making a small donation
  • Early Access to all content!​
